We focus on your health and wellbeing. A gym you can trust!

Our wonderful gym

We offer the perfect balance between classic equipment and the latest innovation to allow users to choose the best route for their goals. We ensure everyone feels welcome when they are in our gym and the facilities ensure our members can exercise in comfort.

Our great classes

We have the a great selection of the latest in innovative training (Les Mills, TRX Suspension Training and more) and traditional classes like Yoga, Spinning and HIIT. Classes to suit all needs and levels.

Personal Trainers

At The 3 Circles Gym we have highly qualified and engaging personal training team, on hand to guide and deliver you through your own custom training plans. Our passionate and supportive trainers will challenge and motivate you to push you to your goals.

Work Out and Get Fit

Here at the 3 Circles Gym we have a variety of different equipement and classes for you to use and to meet your fitness goals. From beginner to advanced classes and workouts, we have you covered.

Feel Great

Our aim is to improve the fitness of our members no matter their background, age or ability. The benefits of regular fitness has been shown to have many health benefits, both physically and mentally.

Positive Mindset

With regular exercise and our support network at the 3 Circles Gym providing expert skills to help you stress less and stay motivated. We will be here to guide you to where you want to go.
